Four Ways to Use AI for Business Research

CLAW provides four distinct modes designed for different research scenarios. Understanding when to use each mode maximizes research effectiveness.

1. Research Mode: Live Web Intelligence

CLAW's Research mode performs live web searches to gather current market data, competitor information, and industry trends. Unlike static AI models trained on historical data, Research mode accesses real-time information from across the internet.

Practical applications include:

  • Monitoring competitor pricing changes and product launches
  • Tracking industry news and regulatory updates
  • Finding potential partners, suppliers, or customers
  • Researching local market conditions and demographics

2. File Analysis Mode: Document Intelligence

File Analysis mode processes PDFs, spreadsheets, and documents to extract insights. Small businesses use this capability to analyze contracts, financial reports, market studies, and competitor materials. CLAW can summarize a 50-page industry report in under 30 seconds.

3. Chat Mode: Strategic Brainstorming

Chat mode provides conversational AI for exploring business ideas, refining strategies, and working through complex decisions. Business owners use Chat mode to pressure-test pricing strategies, draft marketing copy, or explore market entry approaches.

4. Image Analysis Mode: Visual Research

Image Analysis mode interprets visual content including competitor advertisements, product packaging, store layouts, and infographics. Retail businesses particularly benefit from analyzing competitor visual merchandising and brand positioning.

Pro Tip: Combine modes for comprehensive research. Use Research mode to gather competitor URLs, File Analysis to process their downloadable materials, and Chat mode to synthesize findings into actionable recommendations.
