How to Use the CLAW AI Telegram Bot: Complete Guide

By CLAW Team  ·  March 8, 2026  ·  5 min read
Quick Answer

To use the CLAW AI Telegram bot, search for @clawaibot in Telegram and tap Start—no signup required. CLAW provides four modes: Chat, Research with live web search, File Analysis for PDFs and documents, and Image Analysis. The free tier includes 10 uses, and CLAW is powered by Claude AI from Anthropic.

What Is the CLAW AI Telegram Bot?

CLAW is an AI assistant accessible directly through Telegram at @clawaibot. Unlike traditional AI chatbots that require app downloads or account creation, CLAW operates entirely within Telegram's familiar interface. The assistant is powered by Claude AI, Anthropic's advanced language model known for nuanced reasoning and safety.

CLAW serves freelancers, creators, small business owners, and students who need quick AI assistance without switching between applications. According to internal usage data, 73% of CLAW users access the bot during work hours for productivity tasks like research, writing, and document analysis.

The CLAW web application at clawai.app offers the same capabilities, but the Telegram bot provides instant access for users already in the messaging app.

Step-by-Step: How to Use @clawaibot

Step 1: Find and Start the Bot

Open Telegram on any device (mobile, desktop, or web). Search for @clawaibot in the search bar. Tap the bot name, then press the Start button. CLAW responds immediately—no email, password, or verification needed.

Step 2: Choose Your Mode

CLAW offers four distinct modes, each optimized for specific tasks:

  • Chat Mode: General conversation, brainstorming, writing assistance, and coding help
  • Research Mode: Live web search with synthesized answers and source citations
  • File Analysis Mode: Upload PDFs, Word documents, or text files for summarization and Q&A
  • Image Analysis Mode: Send photos or screenshots for visual understanding and data extraction

Type your request naturally, and CLAW automatically selects the appropriate mode. Alternatively, specify the mode directly (e.g., "Research: latest trends in remote work 2026").

Step 3: Send Your Request

For text queries, type your question or task description. For file analysis, attach a document directly in the Telegram chat. For image analysis, send or forward any image. CLAW processes requests in under 3 seconds on average.

Pro Tip: CLAW remembers context within a conversation. Follow-up questions like "summarize that in bullet points" or "translate to Spanish" work without repeating your original request.

Step 4: Iterate and Refine

CLAW excels at multi-turn conversations. Ask for revisions, additional details, or alternative approaches. Each response builds on previous context, making complex tasks manageable through natural dialogue.
