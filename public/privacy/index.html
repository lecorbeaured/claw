Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026

The short version: We collect the minimum data needed to run CLAW. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. Your conversations are used to generate responses and nothing else.

Who We Are

CLAW is an AI assistant operated by EBSS Web Studio ("we," "us," or "our"), based in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. You can reach us at Support.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — if you choose to save your chat history or create an account
  • Conversation content — your messages and any files you upload, used solely to generate AI responses during your session
  • Usage data — anonymous counts of how many queries you've made, stored locally in your browser
  • Payment information — if you subscribe to CLAW Pro, payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details
  • Technical data — standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the CLAW service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, password resets) if you have an account
  • To diagnose technical issues and monitor service health

We do not use your conversations to train AI models. We do not sell your data to any third party.

AI Processing

CLAW uses Claude AI, developed by Anthropic, to generate responses. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to produce replies. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API data. You can review it at anthropic.com/privacy.

We do not retain conversation content after your session ends, unless you have opted in to chat history saving.

Cookies & Local Storage

CLAW uses browser local storage (not cookies) to remember your free usage count and preferences. No tracking cookies, no advertising pixels, no third-party analytics scripts are loaded by default.

Data Sharing

We share data with the following third parties only as needed to operate CLAW:

  • Anthropic — AI response generation
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions
  • Netlify — website hosting and delivery
  • Railway — backend infrastructure

No data is sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any other party.

Data Retention

If you have an account, we retain your email address and subscription status for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing Support. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a common format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at Support.

Children's Privacy

CLAW is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never touches our servers. We apply industry-standard practices to protect data at rest.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of CLAW after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at Support. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
